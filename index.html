WHAT MAKES HAIR COLOR

MC1R

It is a receptor that regulates melanin production and has a great influence on hair color and skin color.

ASIP

It is a protein that is involved in melanogenesis and mainly affects the expression of brown and black pigments.

SLC45A2

It encodes proteins that affect skin color and hair color, and affects changes in skin color and hair color of European people.

TYRP1

A type of enzyme involved in melanin synthesis, mainly related to brown and black hair.

WHAT MAKES EYES COLORS

OCA2

The enzyme involved in melanin synthesis, the more melanin there is, the darker the color of the eyes, and the less melanin there is, the brighter the color of the eyes.

HERC2

It regulates the expression of the OCA2 gene, an enzyme that contributes to melanin synthesis.
